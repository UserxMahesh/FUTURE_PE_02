Content Strategy

Objective:
Increase brand awareness and appointment bookings for Future Salon & Spa.

Target Audience:
- Working professionals
- College students
- Brides-to-be
- Beauty enthusiasts

Content Types:
1. UGC Videos
2. Customer Testimonials
3. Before and After Transformations
4. Beauty Tips and Tutorials
5. Service Demonstrations
6. Seasonal Beauty Campaigns

Platforms:
- Instagram Reels
- Facebook
- YouTube Shorts

Content Goals:
- Increase engagement
- Build trust and credibility
- Showcase salon expertise
- Generate appointment bookings

Call to Action:
Book your appointment today and experience professional beauty and wellness services.

Success Metrics:
- Reach
- Engagement Rate
- Video Views
- Profile Visits
- Appointment Bookings

Marketing Funnel:

Awareness:
UGC videos, beauty tips, short-form content

Consideration:
Testimonials, before-and-after results, service demonstrations

Conversion:
Special offers, appointment booking campaigns, customer reviews
